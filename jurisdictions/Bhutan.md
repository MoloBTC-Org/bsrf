# Bhutan Nation Document  
**Bitcoin Regulation, Self-Custody & Sui Generis Treatment**  
*(Created 23 June 2026)*

**Executive Summary**  
Bhutan has taken a unique approach by leveraging its abundant hydroelectric power for Bitcoin mining. The government has been involved in state-backed Bitcoin mining operations, making it one of the few countries with direct sovereign involvement in Bitcoin mining.

This creates an interesting case study for national energy strategy and Bitcoin.

**1. Historical Thread**  
Bhutan began exploring Bitcoin mining using its surplus hydropower. The government has taken a state-led approach rather than purely private sector development.

**2. Current Regulatory Overview**  
- **Mining**: Government-linked entities have been active in Bitcoin mining using hydroelectric power.
- **Regulation**: Still developing. The focus has been more on mining than on retail trading or self-custody.
- **Taxation**: Specific rules around Bitcoin mining and holdings are still evolving.

**3. Key Players**  
- Royal Government of Bhutan
- Druk Holding and Investments (state investment arm)

**4. Platforms for Advocacy**  
- Direct government engagement
- Energy and mining policy discussions

**5. Philosophical & Natural-Law Synthesis**  
Bhutan’s use of hydropower for Bitcoin mining is a practical example of Bitcoin’s energy infrastructure thesis. It shows how Bitcoin can be integrated with national energy strategy.

**Updated Model Language**  
Recognition of Bitcoin’s role in energy monetisation and infrastructure development is relevant here.

**6. Adoption Implications**  
Bhutan represents a unique model of sovereign Bitcoin mining. Its experience provides valuable insights into national-level Bitcoin strategies.
