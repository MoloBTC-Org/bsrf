## Wamkelekile / Welcome

This document serves as the front door to the Tier 1 foundational papers within the Bitcoin Self-Custody Research Ecosystem. Its purpose is to help readers quickly understand the structure and find the most relevant entry point for their needs. The research is designed to support clear communication and effective collaboration across different roles by providing strong philosophical, legal, and strategic foundations.

### Purpose of Tier 1

Tier 1 establishes the core philosophical, jurisprudential, and strategic foundations for understanding Bitcoin as sui generis bearer property and self-custody as sovereign monetary infrastructure. These papers provide the conceptual grounding upon which the operational guidance in Tier 2 is built.

### Document Structure – Tier 1

Tier 1 consists of three foundational papers:

1. **Bitcoin as Sui Generis Bearer Property** — The core jurisprudential foundation establishing Bitcoin’s unique legal character under Natural Law and Austrian economics.
2. **Bitcoin as Adjacent Currency under International Law** — Analysis of tax treatment, double-tax coordination, and international positioning of self-custodied Bitcoin.
3. **Bitcoin Self-Custody as Sovereign Monetary Infrastructure** — The central integrative synthesis connecting historical patterns of centralised monetary governance, organisational risk, and practical implementation pathways (the primary hub document).

### Navigation

- This Executive Overview & Reading Guide — Primary front door for Tier 1.
- Cross-references throughout the papers support movement between the jurisprudential foundations and the integrative synthesis.
- Each paper contains its own detailed Table of Contents and Truth & Clarity assessment.

### Relationship to Tier 2

Tier 2 (Centralised Monetary Governance, Strategic Risk-Mitigation Framework, and Critical Monetary Infrastructure) builds upon these foundations with expanded historical evidence, detailed risk analysis, and practical self-custody implementation guidance.

### How the Two Tiers Work Together

Tier 1 supplies the jurisprudential and philosophical foundations. Tier 2 applies those principles to concrete historical patterns, organisational risk, and operational self-custody practice. Readers can move fluidly between the two tiers using the cross-references provided in each document. The Executive Overview in each tier explains the recommended reading paths for different roles.

### Recommended Reading Paths

- **Board / Executive level** — Start with the Tier 1 Executive Overview, then the synthesis whitepaper (*Bitcoin Self-Custody as Sovereign Monetary Infrastructure*).
- **Legal / Policy / Compliance** — Begin with *Bitcoin as Sui Generis Bearer Property*, followed by *Bitcoin as Adjacent Currency under International Law*.
- **Technical / Treasury / Security teams** — Move from the synthesis whitepaper into Tier 2 companions for implementation detail.
