# El Salvador Nation Document  
**Bitcoin Regulation, Self-Custody & Sui Generis Treatment**  
*(Created 23 June 2026)*

**Executive Summary**  
El Salvador made history in 2021 by becoming the first country to adopt Bitcoin as legal tender. This makes it a unique and important jurisdiction in the global Bitcoin landscape.

The country has actively promoted Bitcoin adoption, including through the Chivo Wallet and various incentives. Self-custody is fully supported and encouraged.

**1. Historical Thread**  
In September 2021, El Salvador passed the Bitcoin Law, making Bitcoin legal tender alongside the US dollar. The government has since taken various steps to integrate Bitcoin into the economy, including mining using volcanic energy and holding Bitcoin on its balance sheet.

**2. Current Regulatory Overview**  
- **Legal Tender Status**: Bitcoin is legal tender.
- **Taxation**: Generally favourable. No capital gains tax on Bitcoin in many cases when used as legal tender.
- **Self-custody**: Fully supported and promoted.
- **Infrastructure**: Government-backed initiatives alongside private sector development.

**3. Key Players**  
- Government of El Salvador
- Banco Central de Reserva de El Salvador
- Private sector Bitcoin companies and miners

**4. Platforms for Advocacy**  
- Direct engagement with government
- Bitcoin community and events in El Salvador

**5. Philosophical & Natural-Law Synthesis**  
El Salvador’s approach represents one of the most radical real-world experiments in treating Bitcoin as money. It strongly aligns with the sui generis adjacent currency framework and demonstrates the viability of Bitcoin as a parallel monetary system.

**Updated Model Language**  
El Salvador’s legal tender status provides one of the strongest real-world examples of Bitcoin being treated as money alongside state currency.

**6. Adoption Implications**  
El Salvador serves as a powerful case study and inspiration for other jurisdictions considering Bitcoin adoption. Its experience provides valuable lessons on both the opportunities and challenges.

**Next Steps**  
- Monitor ongoing developments in El Salvador’s Bitcoin strategy.
- Document lessons learned for other nations.

**Truth & Clarity Summary**  
El Salvador remains the most significant national-level experiment in Bitcoin adoption. Its legal tender status and supportive policies make it a critical jurisdiction for the global Bitcoin movement.
