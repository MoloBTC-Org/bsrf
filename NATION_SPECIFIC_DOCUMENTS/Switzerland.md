# Switzerland Nation Document  
**Bitcoin Regulation, Self-Custody & Sui Generis Treatment**  
*(Created 23 June 2026)*

**Executive Summary**  
Switzerland, particularly the Canton of Zug ("Crypto Valley"), has established itself as one of the most Bitcoin and crypto-friendly jurisdictions in the world. It has a clear regulatory framework, favourable tax treatment for individuals, and strong support for blockchain innovation.

Self-custody is fully respected, and there is no wealth tax on crypto for private individuals in most cantons.

**1. Historical Thread**  
Switzerland has long been known for its strong property rights, banking privacy tradition, and innovation-friendly environment. Zug began actively courting blockchain companies in the mid-2010s, leading to the development of Crypto Valley.

**2. Current Regulatory Overview**  
- **Regulation**: Clear framework under the Financial Market Infrastructure Act and Anti-Money Laundering Act. Crypto service providers can obtain licences.
- **Taxation**: For private individuals, crypto is generally treated as private wealth. No capital gains tax on private sales of crypto in most cases. Cantonal wealth taxes may apply depending on the canton.
- **Self-custody**: Fully supported and common.

**3. Key Players**  
- Swiss Financial Market Supervisory Authority (FINMA)
- Cantonal tax authorities (especially Zug)
- Blockchain-focused organisations and companies

**4. Platforms for Advocacy**  
- FINMA consultations
- Cantonal policy engagement
- Industry associations (e.g., Crypto Valley Association)

**5. Philosophical & Natural-Law Synthesis**  
Switzerland’s strong tradition of property rights and individual liberty aligns exceptionally well with the sui generis framework. The country’s approach demonstrates that it is possible to have clear regulation while respecting self-custody and innovation.

**Updated Model Language**  
Protective language supporting self-custody and clear tax treatment is highly compatible with Swiss legal traditions.

**6. Adoption Implications**  
Switzerland remains one of the best jurisdictions globally for Bitcoin companies, developers, and holders. Its model is frequently cited as a positive example.

**Truth & Clarity Summary**  
Switzerland is currently one of the most favourable major jurisdictions for Bitcoin. Its combination of regulatory clarity, tax treatment, and cultural support for property rights makes it a model worth studying.
