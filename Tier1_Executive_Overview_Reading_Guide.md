### Wamkelekile / Welcome

This document serves as the front door to the Tier 1 foundational papers within the Bitcoin Self-Custody Research Ecosystem. Its purpose is to help readers quickly understand the structure and find the most relevant entry point for their needs.

### Purpose of Tier 1

Tier 1 establishes the core philosophical, jurisprudential, and strategic foundations for understanding Bitcoin as *sui generis* bearer property and self-custody as sovereign monetary infrastructure.

### Document Structure – Tier 1

Tier 1 consists of three foundational papers:

1. **Bitcoin as Sui Generis Bearer Property** — The core jurisprudential foundation.
2. **Bitcoin as Adjacent Currency under International Law** — Analysis of tax treatment and international positioning.
3. **Bitcoin Self-Custody as Sovereign Monetary Infrastructure** — The central integrative synthesis (historical patterns, risk, and implementation).

### Relationship to Tier 2

Tier 2 (*Centralised Monetary Governance*, *Strategic Risk-Mitigation Framework*, and *Critical Monetary Infrastructure*) builds upon these foundations with historical evidence, risk analysis, and practical implementation guidance.

### Recommended Reading Paths

- **Board / Executive level** — Start with this Executive Overview, then the synthesis whitepaper.
- **Legal / Policy** — Begin with *Sui Generis Bearer Property* and *Adjacent Currency*.
- **Technical / Treasury teams** — Move from the synthesis whitepaper into Tier 2 companions.
