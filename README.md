## Purpose

This repository serves as the canonical **Single Source of Truth** for the Bitcoin Sovereignty Research Framework. It establishes and maintains a coherent body of work that treats Bitcoin as **sui generis bearer property** and an **adjacent currency** to state-issued money.

The framework integrates deep jurisprudential foundations with practical operational guidance, risk analysis, and implementation pathways. It is designed to support sovereign individuals, policymakers, legal practitioners, researchers, and technical implementers.

Core positions maintained throughout this repository:
- Bitcoin is **sui generis** and cannot be coherently forced into traditional legal categories.
- Self-custody and the Right to Noding are fundamental rights.
- Taxation and regulatory measures should apply only to realised economic activity with a clear territorial nexus.
- A clear distinction must be maintained between Bitcoin and issuer-dependent crypto assets.

## Two-Tier Architecture

The framework is deliberately structured in two interconnected tiers.

### Tier 1 – Foundations
Philosophical, jurisprudential, and historical foundations.

**Primary documents**:
- `Bitcoin_Sui_Generis_Bearer_Property_Whitepaper_v1.md`
- `Bitcoin_Adjacent_Currency_International_Law_Whitepaper_v1.md`
- `Global_Principles_v1.md`

These documents establish the core intellectual position of the framework.

### Tier 2 – Operational
Risk, implementation, infrastructure, and practical application.

**Primary documents** (located in `operational/`):
- Strategic Risk Mitigation Framework
- Critical Monetary Infrastructure guidance
- Implementation Pathways and Centralised Monetary Governance analysis

Tier 2 documents are explicitly grounded in the principles established in Tier 1.
